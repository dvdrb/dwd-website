3. How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard website typically takes 4–8 weeks from initial consultation to launch. More complex projects like custom web applications may take longer. We’ll provide you with a detailed timeline after the project scope is defined.

4. How much does a website cost?

Costs vary based on your specific needs — the number of pages, design complexity, special features (like e-commerce, custom forms, integrations), and more. After a free consultation, we’ll send you a personalized quote.

5. Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we build are fully responsive, meaning they look great and work seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

6. Do you provide website maintenance and support after launch?

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website updated, secure, and running smoothly. This includes regular backups, security updates, content updates, and technical support.

7. Can you redesign my existing website?

Of course. We can update or completely redesign your existing website to improve its performance, user experience, and appearance while keeping your brand identity intact.

8. Do you offer SEO services?

Yes, we integrate basic SEO best practices into every website we build. We also offer advanced SEO packages, including keyword research, on-page optimization, and SEO audits to help boost your site's visibility on search engines.
