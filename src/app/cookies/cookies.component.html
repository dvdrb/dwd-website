Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when you visit our website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as language preferences, font size, and other display settings.

Cookies are widely used to make websites function more efficiently and to provide information to site owners. They enhance user experience and help personalize content.
